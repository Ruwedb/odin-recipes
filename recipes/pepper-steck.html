Pepper Steak

Discription

Satisfy your barbeque cravings with this easy and flavoursome lemon pepper steak recipe. Add salad, mash, or greens for a wholesome meal that's ideal for any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp cracked black pepper
  • 1/3 cup finely chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley leaves
  • 1 tbsp finely grated lemon rind
  • 1/3 cup horseradish cream
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 8 (150g each) beef scotch fillet steaks
  • olive oil cooking spray

Steps

  1. Combine pepper, parsley and lemon rind on a plate. Combine horseradish and lemon juice in a bowl.
  2. Brush 1 side of each steak with horseradish mixture. Press same side into pepper mixture. Repeat with remaining horseradish and pepper mixtures.
  3. Preheat a barbecue plate or chargrill on medium-high. Spray steaks with oil. Cook for 2 minutes, seasoning side down. Turn steaks and cook for 2 minutes or until cooked to your liking. Transfer to a platter. Cover loosely with foil. Set aside for 5 minutes to rest. Serve.
